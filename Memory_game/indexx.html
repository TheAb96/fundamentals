Instructions

Concenration, also known as Match Match,Memory,Plemanism,Shinkei-suljaku,Pexeso or simply Paris , is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn, the Object of the game to turn over Paris of matching cards
